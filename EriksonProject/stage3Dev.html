Iniative vs. Guilt

1st: My first Halloween costume.
2nd: Me plucking a few flowers after having explored the yard

Explanation

By this time, I was looking forward to start trick or treating as I had seen my cousins and sister do so before. I chose my own costume to wear that night and was very happy that I was able to do some of the things I wanted. Additionally, I started exploring my home's massive yard a couple of times on my own whim, plucking out some flowers and plants I found interesting. Being able to have that freedom to start exploring was exciting for me, but at times I was anxious of the occasional scoldings, especially if I got dirty.

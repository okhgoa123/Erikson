About

Welcome to my Erikson's Project! There are two ways to navigate through my project:

Developmental

The developmental mode will take you from stage to stage. In order to represent development, the website will "evolve" or develop, starting out as a plain and basic page but gradually grows in structure and style with each new stage, eventually becoming a well-polished website.

Timeline

The timeline mode will lay out all the stages of Erikson's in a well-polished fashion without having to go through each stage individually. To see more details about each stage, click on the section.
